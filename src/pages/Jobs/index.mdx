# Careers at Carapace

Carapace is building decentralized protection against default risk in crypto loans. Our vision is to reimagine the way the world manages credit and we aim to become the largest credit risk protection protocol in DeFi.

We were founded within the [South Park Commons program](https://www.southparkcommons.com/), and backed by world-class VCs and angels. We are hiring for founding team members - this is an opportunity to work at the cutting edge of crypto and financial markets. Come join us as one of the earliest members of the team where you’ll help shape the vision, strategy and culture of this protocol.
