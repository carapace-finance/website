# Senior Full-Stack Blockchain Engineer

We are looking for a senior full-stack blockchain engineer to join our core engineering team and own significant components of our backend and frontend systems. You will have autonomy to design, build, and scale our system.

## Responsibilities

- Smart contract development - you will design, develop, test, and ship backend features.
- Dapp development - you will design, develop, test, and ship new frontend features.
- Interacting with users - you will be interacting with and support our community of users through our documentation,  blog and discord server.

## Ideal Qualifications

- Experience with building a DeFi protocol in production
- Good understanding of finance, especially the credit industry
- Good understanding of DeFi protocols

## Requirements

- 3-5+ years software engineering experience
- Experience in Solidity and Typescript
- Understanding of blockchain technology

## Perks and Benefits

We’re a globally distributed team that is remote-friendly. We provide:

- Competitive salary and equity
- Healthcare benefits — medical/dental/vision for U.S. based employees, stipends for non-U.S. based employees

To apply to this position, please send us a message at tai@carapace.finance or @tai_telegram with details about your past work. e.g. live apps, GitHub repos, a resume.
